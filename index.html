About Dhar Iiwiya

Heritage meets luxury in Somali fashion

قصة دار عيوة

Dhar Iiwiya was founded in 2010 with a vision to redefine Somali traditional fashion. We believe that cultural heritage and contemporary luxury can coexist, creating pieces that honor tradition while embracing modern aesthetics.

Our name, "Dhar Iiwiya," translates to "clothing of excellence" - a promise we uphold in every stitch, every fabric choice, and every design we create. Each piece in our collection is meticulously crafted by master artisans who have inherited their skills through generations.

Our Philosophy

We combine the rich textures and patterns of Somali culture with luxurious fabrics and contemporary silhouettes. Every garment tells a story of heritage, craftsmanship, and elegance.

Our Commitment

From ethically sourced materials to sustainable production practices, we are committed to excellence at every level. We work directly with Somali weavers and artisans to support local communities.
