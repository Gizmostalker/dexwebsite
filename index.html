Our Story

The Beginning

DEX was founded by Alexander Reed in a small London studio in 2010. With a vision to create timeless luxury clothing that transcends fleeting trends, our first collection featured just 12 meticulously crafted pieces. The immediate attention from fashion critics and luxury shoppers marked the beginning of our journey.

First Flagship Store

In 2013, we opened our first flagship store on London's prestigious Bond Street. The store's elegant interior, designed by award-winning architect Maria Chen, became a destination in itself. This expansion established DEX as a serious contender in the global luxury fashion market.

International Expansion

By 2016, DEX had expanded to Paris, Milan, and New York. Our international collections were featured in Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, and other leading fashion publications. Each new location brought our unique blend of traditional craftsmanship and contemporary design to fashion capitals worldwide.

Sustainability Initiative

In 2019, we launched our comprehensive sustainability program, committing to ethical sourcing, eco-friendly materials, and responsible manufacturing processes. We partner with artisans and suppliers who share our values, ensuring every piece tells a story of quality and conscience.
