Every DEX garment tells a story of heritage, quality, and uncompromising attention to detail. From the selection of premium fabrics to the final stitch, we ensure excellence at every step.

About DEX

Redefining Somali luxury fashion

قصة علامة ديكس

DEX was established in 2012 with a singular mission: to create the world's finest Somali traditional fashion. We believe that cultural heritage and contemporary luxury are not mutually exclusive, but rather can be woven together to create something truly extraordinary.

The name "DEX" symbolizes excellence, precision, and luxury. It represents our commitment to creating garments that are not just clothing, but works of art that celebrate Somali culture while meeting the highest international standards of quality and design.

Our Craftsmanship

Each DEX piece is handcrafted by master artisans who have honed their skills over decades. We work with the finest materials sourced from around the world, including premium silks, Egyptian cottons, and hand-woven fabrics.

Our Vision

To become the global benchmark for luxury Somali fashion, creating pieces that are cherished for generations while continuously innovating and pushing the boundaries of traditional design.
